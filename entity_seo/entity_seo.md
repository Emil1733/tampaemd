# Entity SEO Strategy: poolremovaltampa.com

### 1. What is Entity SEO for Tampa Pool Removal?
Entity SEO is the process of building **"Authority Density"** for `poolremovaltampa.com` by referencing interconnected topics that Google’s algorithms recognize as essential to the Tampa pool demolition industry.

*   **The Shift:** Google doesn't just want to see the keyword "pool removal." It wants to see that we understand the **Tampa Knowledge Graph**: high water tables, sandy soil compaction, Hillsborough County permitting, and subtropical drainage.
*   **The "Expert" Analogy:** A general contractor knows how to break concrete. A **Tampa Pool Expert** knows about dewatering systems, sinkhole risks in Carrollwood, and why "clean fill" requirements vary between Tampa and St. Pete.

### 2. How Google Measures Our Authority
Google uses two primary signals to measure our entity relationship to the Tampa market:

1.  **Sitemap Depth:** The total list of specific "sub-entities" we cover (e.g., Fiberglass vs. Concrete, Partial vs. Full).
2.  **Entity Proximity:** How closely related our pages are. 
    *   **Low Score (Good):** "Pool Removal" + "High Water Table Stabilization" + "South Tampa Drainage."
    *   **High Score (Bad):** "Pool Removal" + "General Landscaping" + "Kitchen Remodeling." (This dilutes our expert status).

### 3. Tampa-Specific Implementation
To rank #1 for "Pool Removal Tampa," we must cover the entities Google expects from a local authority:

*   **Technical Sub-Entities:** Create dedicated pages/sections for:
    *   **The Water Table Problem:** Why dewatering is mandatory in South Tampa.
    *   **Soil Stabilization:** Managing sandy soil and preventing sinkholes post-fill.
    *   **Material Entities:** Fiberglass pool "popping" risks vs. Concrete bond-beam cracking.
*   **Location Entities (Neighborhoods):** 
    *   Google recognizes **South Tampa, Brandon, Clearwater, St. Petersburg, and Carrollwood** as entities belonging to the larger Tampa market.
    *   Individual landing pages for these areas prove we are a "Local Authority," not just a national lead-gen site.
*   **Regulatory Entities:** Detailed guides on **Hillsborough County vs. Pinellas County** permits.
